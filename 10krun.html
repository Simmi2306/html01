10K Run

A 10K run is a long-distance road race that covers a distance of 10 kilometers or 6.2 miles. It's a popular race distance for runners of all levels, and is often a next step for runners who have completed a 5K race:
Distance:A 10K is twice the distance of a 5K race.
Difficulty: A 10K is a good challenge without being as daunting as a marathon.
Popularity: A 10K is one of the most common types of road running events.
Training: Proper training and preparation can help runners achieve their goals.
Next steps: A 10K can be part of a larger training plan for a half marathon or full marathon.
