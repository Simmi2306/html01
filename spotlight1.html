HDFC Bank Savethon

HDFC Bank today organised the 1st edition of Saveathon – a run with a noble cause, in the city. Saveathon aims to create awareness about water scarcity with an objective to encourage people to reduce wastage and increase conservation. HDFC Bank Saveathon was flagged off by Mayor Shri Bonthu Rammohan from People’s Plaza at Necklace Road and followed landmarks such Hussain Sagar lake. 6,000 people of Hyderabad participated in HDFC Bank Saveathon and took a pledge to save water. The run had two categories - 5 and 10 kilometres.
